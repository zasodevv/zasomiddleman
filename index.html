Middle Man

A middleman is an intermediary who facilitates transactions between a buyer and a seller, helping to streamline the exchange of goods, services, or information. Middlemen can take various forms, such as wholesalers, agents, brokers, or retailers, and they perform essential functions like logistics, marketing, and communication. By connecting producers with consumers, middlemen play a crucial role in ensuring that transactions are completed efficiently and effectively, often adding value through their expertise and networks.

Exchange

An exchange service for money is a platform or business that facilitates the trading of one currency for another. These services can include currency exchange offices, banks, online platforms, and cryptocurrency exchanges. They enable individuals and businesses to convert money from one currency to another, which is essential for international travel, trade, and investment. Examples include services like Forex (foreign exchange markets), PayPal, Western Union, and cryptocurrency exchanges like Coinbase and Binance.
